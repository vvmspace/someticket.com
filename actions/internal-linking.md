# Prompt: internal linking and navigation

Design the internal linking system for `someticket.com`, a ticket aggregation website.

Constraints:

- Major entity hubs may live at the root level.
- Intersections may live at deeper URL levels.
- Event pages live under `/events/`.
- The site must scale to large numbers of events from many aggregators.
- The internal linking model must support content clusters, discoverability, and conversion paths.

Cover:

1. Breadcrumb logic by page type.
2. Related links logic for hubs.
3. Related links logic for landing pages.
4. Related links logic for events.
5. Rules for featured links vs automatic links.
6. Rules to avoid orphan pages.
7. Rules for nearby entities and sibling entities.
8. Footer, sidebar, and in-content linking opportunities.
9. Canonical and noindex guidance for thin intersections.
10. Suggested data structures or front matter fields to support this.

Write the answer as a practical technical specification in English.
